Our SaaS platform offers three pricing tiers: Starter ($29/month), Professional ($79/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing). Each tier includes different levels of support and features.

The Starter plan includes up to 5 team members, 10GB storage, email support, and basic analytics. It is ideal for small teams getting started.

The Professional plan includes up to 25 team members, 100GB storage, priority email and chat support, advanced analytics, API access, and custom integrations. It is best for growing businesses.

The Enterprise plan includes unlimited team members, unlimited storage, 24/7 phone support, dedicated account manager, custom SLAs, SSO/SAML authentication, and advanced security features.

To upgrade your plan, go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. You can upgrade at any time and the cost will be prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

For password resets, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your registered email. The link expires after 24 hours.

To invite team members, go to Settings > Team > Invite. Enter their email address and select a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). They will receive an invitation email to join your workspace.
